How a Build Runs

Site to strike, in five stages.

The same sequence whether it's a three-day exhibition stall or a thousand-guest wedding tent — only the scale changes.

01

Site Survey

We measure ground, power load, access routes and local permissions before a single pole is ordered.

02

Layout & Design

Span, height and stall plans are drawn to spec and signed off with you before fabrication.

03

Build & Rig

Frame, cladding, flooring, power and lighting go up in sequence — usually overnight, ahead of your event day.

04

Live Support

Our crew stays on-site through the event for cooling, power and structural checks — no handoff, no guesswork.

05

Strike

Full teardown and site clearance, usually completed within hours of your event's close.
